About this Portfolio

❀

The purpose of this portfolio is to show what I learned during the term and how I have improved my English. It also helps me reflect on my progress and understand my learning better.

During this process, I learned the importance of using digital tools in an ethical way, being responsible with information and respecting other people's work.

Throughout the term, I developed skills like reading, writing, and understanding English, as well as communicating my ideas more clearly. I also improved my digital skills by creating and organizing my work using different tools.

This portfolio will help me in my future studies and in the workplace because it makes me more confident, organized, and better at using English. Also, presenting this portfolio helps me trust more in myself and lose my fear of speaking in front of others.

Cycle 01

Cycle One

Beginning the Journey

✦ ♡ ✦

Cycle Overview

During the first term, we worked on different topics in English that helped us improve our grammar and communication skills. We learned about relative clauses, including defining and non-defining clauses, and how to use them correctly in sentences. We also studied reported speech and how to change direct sentences into indirect ones.

In addition, we reviewed basic sentence structure, like subject, verb, and complement, and practiced how to organize ideas clearly. We also learned about classroom rules and the importance of responsibility, meeting deadlines, and using our time wisely.

All these activities helped us develop our writing, reading, and understanding of English, as well as our ability to communicate more effectively.

Cycle Two

Sources Organization & References

✦ ♡ ✦

Cycle Overview

During the second cycle, we watched the movie Her, which is about artificial intelligence and human relationships. After watching it, we answered different questions to analyze the story, the characters, and the message of the film.

We also identified three problems related to the use of AI in the movie, which helped us think more deeply about its impact on people's emotions and daily life.

This activity helped us develop critical thinking skills and express our opinions in English, while also reflecting on the ethical use of technology.

Cycle 03

Cycle Three

Mock Test Season

✦ ♡ ✦

Cycle Overview

During the third cycle, we worked on a mock IELTS test as the main summative activity of the cycle. The goal was to evaluate our English level in skills such as Listening, Reading, and Writing and also prepare us for the real test.

During the cycle, we also learned strategies to improve our English, such as reading faster, identifying key information, and organizing our ideas more clearly.

Personally, my work is incomplete because part of the test was done on a day when I was not present. However, this situation taught me an important lesson: I need to always ask what was done in class when I miss a day.

Even with this difficulty, the experience helped me improve my academic English and better understand how to prepare for exams like IELTS.

Cycle 05

Cycle Five

Campaign Time

✦ ♡ ✦

Cycle Overview

During the fifth cycle, we worked on creating an AI Awareness Campaign as a group. This was based on the problems we had identified in the second term after watching the movie Her.

We started by choosing one of those problems, and in our case, we focused on deepfakes. Then, we created a draft of a campaign to raise awareness about this issue in the school community.

To do this, we used academic research to support our ideas and learned how to communicate our message in a clear and persuasive way. We also practiced using reported speech and conditional sentences to explain our arguments better.

This project helped us understand the importance of using technology ethically and how to inform others about the risks of artificial intelligence in a responsible way.

Cycle Six

Campaign Reflection Presentation

✦ ♡ ✦

Cycle Overview

During the sixth cycle, we continued working on our AI Awareness Campaign. We focused on organizing our research and improving our ideas to prepare a clear and structured presentation.

♡ Special Project

Campaign

AI & Deepfakes Awareness

❀ ✦ ♡ ✦ ❀

Campaign Overview

This campaign was created to raise awareness about the dangers of deepfakes and the misuse of artificial intelligence. Our main goal was to help people understand that not everything they see online is real and to encourage them to verify information before sharing it.

During the campaign, we used posters, brochures, and interactive activities to explain the topic in a simple and engaging way. Many people showed interest and participated in the activities, which helped them learn how to identify fake content.

Overall, the campaign was successful because it informed others, promoted critical thinking, and made people more aware of digital risks.
